My Values

I value honesty and integrity, trust, loyalty, love, and human relationships. One assignment was Model the Way, which helped define and narrow down what I value in others and myself. I value honesty and integrity because honesty has gotten me farther in life. It leads to people trusting me and me trusting in myself and others. Trust shows others that I am loyal others through my word or promises and actions, and who is loyal to me. Moreover, loyalty gives give love to my relationships and friendship, which I take very seriously, and without the value of love there would be on relationship in the first place. Lastly, I value human relationships that are created from the love because we are here to find our better half to bring happiness to life.
